I Love Tea

I'm an avid tea drinker who loves talking about tea with other tea lovers. Visit my tumblr "Sipping Tea, Watching Ducks." Or you can follow me on Instagram for tea pics. Here are a few of my favorite teas:

Darjeeling

This is probably my all-time favorite. I would like to visit Darjeeling, India someday.

Lapsang

Smokey lapsang tea reminds me of sitting by a campfire in the woods.

Dianhong

The perfect black tea on a weekend morning.

Earl Grey

Earl Grey is a fail-safe choice, especially on a rainy day.

Jasmine

I turn to jasmine when I want a green tea with a little character.

Sencha

Sencha is a classic Japanese tea that provides calm and serenity.
